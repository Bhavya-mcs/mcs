About Our Firm

Manibhadra CNC Solutions, established in 2021 and led by Asit Mehta, is dedicated to providing high-quality CNC job work for interior designers, architects, and private clients. We use our advanced machinery to bring complex and artistic visions to life, specializing in religious and decorative carvings.

We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail and ability to execute custom designs with precision. Our work includes custom temple art, intricate wall panels, religious statues, and more, all crafted with care and expertise.
